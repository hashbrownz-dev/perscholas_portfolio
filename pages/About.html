An Interesting Fact

Before the pandemic, I worked for an event company that created ice sculptures and water features. Below are videos of two projects I was apart of.

In the first clip, we partnered with Super Deluxe to see if we could create a functioning bong out of ice. I programmed and operated the CNC machine that cut the silhouette of the swan in two halves. You can briefly see a younger version of myself freezing the two halves together and putting them back in the freezer around the 1 minute mark.

In the second clip, I had the privilege of attending the 2019 BET Music Awards, installing and operating a digital water curtain. This project was a lot of fun, because they gave me complete creative control over the design of the water show.
